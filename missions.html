Tracy Evans Ministry

Africa 180° is dedicated to reversing the cycles of poverty, disease and death in Mozambique, Africa. We do this in word and in deed, as we proclaim the Gospel of Jesus Christ to those we live with and minister to. Through our clinic and nutrition program, we treat 900 orphan infants, HIV+ children plus their care-givers. We have a hospice/recovery center, a pre-school and kindergarten, two prison ministries, 22 church plants, leadership classes for government officials, and pastors, and a farming program.

Julie & Nate Miller

Julie is the founder and director of Mussunza preschool, a preschool that serves a rural community of Mozambique and was established to provide quality childhood education in an underprivileged region. The preschool’s principle objective is to address the well-being of the whole child while fostering creativity, hope, love and knowledge of the Father. The learning program centers on aca- demics, spiritual growth, health and nutrition, as well as social and emotional development. The school is staffed by local Mozambicans who share in a desire to help children realize their potential and become tomorrow’s leaders. Julie married Nate Miller in June of 2012. Nate has a background in project management and international development.
