Giving Options

1. What are the different ways to give to Bethel Church?

You can give to Bethel Church using any of the following systems:
- Give by credit card or electronic check at give.ibethel.org
- Give by credit card using NEW Text to Give (see instructions - below)
- Give by check by mailing it to us (see instructions below)
- Give by cash or check during one of our weekend services

2. How do I give to Bethel Church online using a credit card or checking account?

NOTE: Make sure you are using the newest version of Internet Explorer, Google Chrome, or Mozilla Firefox web browsers
- Go to give.ibethel.org.
Select the fund you would like to donate towards and press “Give”
- Select whether you are donating from the “United States” or “International”
- Login using your Church Login ID (this is different from your Bethel.TV and Bethel Store login). Please see question 5 below if you don’t know this information
- Once logged in, update your personal contact information, and then click “Next”
- Decide whether you would like to the donation to be “one-time” or “recurring”
- Fill in the amount you would like to give next to the fund(s) you want to donate towards, and then click “Next”
- Select whether you want to give by Credit Card or ACH
- Fill in your payment information, click “Next”, and then confirm your donation on the final page

NOTE: The first time you use Mobile Giving you will need to follow a link to enter your credit card information. After that, you can donate anytime by a simple text message!

3. How do I give by text message using a credit card?

Text to Give is the newest and quickest way to donate to Bethel Church! Just follow these 3 easy steps:

Step 1: Start a new text message to the number 45777
Step 2: Enter the $ amount you want to give followed by a giving keyphrase below
Step 3: Send text message

NOTE: The first time you use Mobile Giving you will need to follow a link to enter your credit card information. After that, you can donate anytime by a simple text message!

Available Keyphrases
“Bethel Give” for Tithes & Offerings
“Bethel Missions” for General Missions
“Bethel Vision” for Vision 2022 Building Fund

EXAMPLE: Texting “100 Bethel Give” to 45777 will be a $100.00 donation to Tithe and Offerings.

4. How do I give to Bethel Church by mailing a check?

Please make all checks payable to “Bethel Church” and write what you are giving towards in the memo line (Tithe, General Offering, Missions, Etc). Our mailing address is:

5. What if I don’t know my give.ibethel.org username and/or password?

Create New Account - If you have never used the Giving Site before, then you will need to create a new account by clicking here.

Forgot My Username - If you or your spouse have used the Giving Site before, but don’t remember your Username, you can have it emailed to you by clicking here.

Forgot My Password - If you don’t remember your password, you can reset it by clicking here.

6. What if the giving page times out or crashes while trying to donate?

Make sure you are accessing the giving page using the newest version of Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox, or Google Chrome. (Safari or other browsers are not supported at this time).

If you are already using one of the browsers above, please email us at giving@ibethel.org and let us know your name, the amount you are trying to donate, and a brief description of the error you are receiving (screenshots also help), and we will contact you as soon as possible.

7. When will my Bethel Giving Statement be sent out?

2014 Giving Statements will be sent on or before January 30, 2015.

8. How will I receive my Bethel Giving Statement?

Giving Statements will be emailed out if we have your email in our database.

If we do not have your email address on file or you have requested to receive a paper statement, we will mail your Giving Statement to the address we have on file on or before January 30, 2015 (UNITED STATES ONLY).

If you are an International, and need a copy of your Giving Statement, then please email us after January 30, 2015 at giving@ibethel.org to request an emailed copy.

9. What if I don’t receive my Bethel Giving Statement by mid-February?

If you haven’t received your statement by email or regular mail by mid-February then it’s possible we have outdated contact information for you. If this happens, please email us at giving@ibethel.org with your full name, mailing address and email, and we will send it to you as soon as possible.

10. If I mail my contribution in December 2014, but it doesn’t reach my church until January 2015, which year should I claim it?

On your 2014 tax return. A check that is mailed to a charity is deductible in the year that it was mailed and postmarked, even if it is received in the next fiscal year.

11. If I make a contribution in early January 2015, can I claim it on my 2014 taxes?

No. Charitable contributions must be claimed in the year in which they are donated.

EXAMPLE: Jessica writes a check to her church on December 31, 2014, and deposits it in the church offering on Sunday, January 5, 2015. In this case, her check is NOT deductible on her 2014 taxes and will not be included on her 2014 Bethel Church Giving Statement. She can claim it in the following year.
