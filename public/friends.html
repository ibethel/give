Global Legacy

Global Legacy is a relational network that connects, encourages, and equips revival leaders worldwide. Ministries, churches, organizations, and individuals come together through an online resource that provides video conferencing, leadership training, social networking, and opportunity for connection with others in their region or area of influence.

Iris Ministries

Rolland and Heidi Baker are the founding directors of Iris Ministries, which began in 1980. They arrived in Mozambique in 1995, after ministering for twelve years in Asia and three years in the UK. They began to pour out their lives and hearts among the street children in Mozambique. Iris Ministries now oversees fifteen bases worldwide and has planted thousands of churches.

Moral Revolution

Moral Revolution is a purity movement. The world has screamed perverse messages of love, lust, beauty and sexuality through all forms of media, while the church has remained predominantly silent. It is our goal to begin discussing and releasing freedom regarding these "shamed" topics with kingdom perspective! The church should be the most safe, empowering environment when it comes to modeling what love should look like. God gave us our sex drive. It is our job to learn to manage it!
